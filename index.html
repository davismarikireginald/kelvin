About the Convocation

The 7th convocation of Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community. It is the time to change views on topical issues that touch teaching, research and professional development.

African Statistical Day is celebrated each year on 18th of November. The commemoration aims among others, to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development. This year’s convocation aims to understand the African Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and the potential role statistics and big data plays in discovering markets and serving customers across Africa.

Convocation Schedule

Date: 19th November 2024

Time: 08:00-16:00

Location:Eastern Statistical Training center

Agenda:This year’s convocation has selected the topic “The role of official statistics and big data in the economy transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania“ as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year’s 18 of November African Statistics Day. The theme states “Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Data Area (AfCFTA): The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Africa“
